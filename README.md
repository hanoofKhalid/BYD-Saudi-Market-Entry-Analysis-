## 🚢 The Silk Road to Riyadh: Zero-Dependency Logistics
* **Supply Chain Resilience:** BYD utilizes its proprietary RoRo fleet (9,200 vehicles/ship) for total logistical control from Shenzhen to Jeddah Islamic Port[span_10](start_span)[span_10](end_span).
* **Regional Hubs:** Aligning with Saudi’s *Global Supply Chain Resilience Initiative (GSCRI)* to establish regional distribution infrastructure for the GCC[span_11](start_span)[span_11](end_span).

* **Al-Futtaim Partnership:** Rapid network expansion targeting 10 fully-integrated 3S (Sales, Service, Spare Parts) facilities nationwide by **H2 2026**[span_12](start_span)[span_12](end_span).
